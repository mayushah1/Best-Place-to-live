California Fun Facts

When you're moving, it's important to know what you're getting in to. But before you actually live in a neighborhood, it can be hard to get a feeling for the kind of community you're choosing. So we've put together some data for you to play around with to learn more about where you're going to move!

Median Income by Education Level

This chart displays the median income of people over 25 years old within each school district in California, broken down by level of education. You can also select "Men" or "Women" to view only those stats.

Important! Keep in mind while viewing the chart that the Y-axis changes significantly depending on which dataset you're viewing.

Source: American FactFinder (2016)

Public vs Private Schools

This chart displays the percentage of children (3 years old-high school) enrolled in public or private school in each school district in California.

When interpreting this chart, please keep in mind that the percentages should be interpreted in context with the total number of students in each district. To help with this, we've included the total number of students for each school district, broken up by public/private, as well as a separate chart below showing the total enrollment.

Source: American FactFinder (2016)
